Uma Public School boats of 2500 students with a sprawling campus set against an idyllic landscape of 12 acres, situated in Maincha, Greater Noida offers convenient accessibility to Central and East Delhi, Noida and Ghaziabad.

At UMA PUBLIC SCHOOL, we're redefining education with our Real World Curriculum™. Our approach goes beyond textbooks, fostering well-rounded students ready to excel in life.
